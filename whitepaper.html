InsiderJobs Nigeria

Connecting talented Nigerians with hidden job opportunities

Overview

InsiderJobs is a platform designed to help job seekers in Nigeria gain access to exclusive job listings that are often never posted publicly. We work directly with HR departments, startups, and local businesses to list openings before they go mainstream.

Mission

To reduce unemployment by bridging the gap between hidden job opportunities and skilled Nigerians ready to work.

Key Features

  • Verified Insider Listings
  • Real-time Job Alerts
  • Application Tracking
  • Location Job Matching

How It Works

Step 1: Create your profile and upload your resume.
Step 2: Receive alerts on verified insider job posts.
Step 3: Apply with one click.
Step 4: Track your application status live.

Why Nigeria?

Nigeria has a fast-growing youth population and one of the largest labor forces in Africa, yet unemployment remains high. InsiderJobs focuses specifically on this gap by surfacing hard-to-find roles across all 36 states.

Vision

To become the #1 insider job discovery platform in Africa by 2030.
