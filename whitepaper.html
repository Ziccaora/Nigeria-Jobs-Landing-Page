LocalRoute. Nigeria

Empowering drivers and optimizing transportation across Nigeria

Overview

LocalRoute is a decentralized driver management platform built to revolutionize transportation safety and route optimization in Nigeria. We connect drivers, businesses, and commuters through smart technology, making mobility safer, smarter, and more efficient.

Mission

To build the largest and safest driver network in Africa by empowering drivers and protecting commuters through innovation and transparency.

Key Features

  • Driver Safety Verification
  • Emergency SOS System
  • State and LGA-based Navigation
  • Smart Route Matching

How It Works

Step 1: Drivers register, and verify identity.
Step 2: Drivers get matched to optimal routes and opportunities.
Step 3: Emergency SOS ensures safety during journeys.

Why Nigeria?

Nigeria's booming transportation sector faces growing challenges in driver management and commuter safety. LocalRoute focuses on modernizing the system by connecting drivers to technology and expanding access to safer, optimized routes across the 36 states and 774 LGAs.

Vision

To become Africa's #1 decentralized driver management platform by 2030.
