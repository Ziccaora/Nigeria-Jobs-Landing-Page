Security & Privacy

At Okija, we take security and privacy seriously. Our platform is built with the following security measures:

End-to-End Encryption

All communications are encrypted using industry-standard protocols, ensuring that only the intended recipients can read your messages.

Privacy Controls

Granular privacy settings allow you to control who can see your profile, status, and when you were last online.

Data Protection

We adhere to strict data protection policies and never sell your data to third parties. Your information remains yours.

Group Features

Okija offers powerful group management capabilities for communities, teams, and organizations:

  • Create groups with up to 1000 members
  • Assign multiple administrators with different permission levels
  • Set group rules and guidelines
  • Broadcast messages to all group members
  • Group analytics and engagement reports
  • File sharing with version control
  • Pinned messages and announcements
  • Custom group invites and join requests
